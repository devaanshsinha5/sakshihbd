My dearest Sakshi,

There are men who have waged holy wars for far lesser things — kingdoms of sand, thrones of iron, names carved into stone. I would wage mine quietly, in the dark, for nothing more than the right to hear your laugh one more time. That is the nature of what you have done to me.

525 days. Not a single one wasted. Not a single one I would trade. You turned what I thought was a perfectly good life into proof that I had no idea what a good life looked like.

You are the kind of person poets wrote letters to before they had addresses. The kind of grace that makes a man want to be worth it — not for the world, not for ambition — but just to stand next to you and not feel undeserving.

So on this day, the twenty-third year of you existing on this earth, I want you to know: I am grateful. For every version of you. The soft ones, the stubborn ones, the ones that show up at 2am with an opinion and no apology.

Happy birthday, jaan. The world is better and stranger and more beautiful because you are in it.

yours, always —Devaansh

The Office of Devaansh Sinha

Contract of Undying Loyalty

and Absolute, Non-Negotiable Devotion

Preamble

This legally binding, emotionally enforceable, and spiritually certified document hereby establishes the terms and conditions governing the relationship between Devaansh Sinha (hereinafter referred to as "The Devoted One," "Your Person," or "That Guy Who Loves You Embarrassingly Much") and Sakshi Singh (hereinafter referred to as "The Love of His Life," "The Prettiest One in Every Room," or simply "Baby").

Article I — Loyalty

Section 1.1: The Devoted One hereby declares, under penalty of having to watch a sad movie together and cry about it, that his loyalty to The Love of His Life is total, unconditional, and frankly a little embarrassing to admit in public. He has tried to imagine a version of himself that is not completely devoted to her. It did not compute.

Section 1.2: Said loyalty shall not expire, depreciate, or diminish upon the occurrence of any of the following: bad moods, long distances, sleepy arguments, or the fact that she sometimes steals the blanket.

Article II — Obligations of The Devoted One

The Devoted One agrees, without coercion (mostly), to the following:

  1. To love her loudly when she needs it, and quietly when she needs that more.
  2. To be the first person she calls when things go wrong and the one she tells first when things go right.
  3. To take her side in all matters, except when she is objectively wrong, in which case he will take her side anyway and tell her privately.
  4. To show up. Always. In every way that counts.
  5. To think she is beautiful on every day, including and especially on the days she does not believe it herself.
  6. To be patient with her, grow with her, and never stop choosing her.
  7. To buy her things from the wishlist (Gift 1) at earliest financially viable opportunity.

Article III — Duration & Termination

This contract shall remain in force for the duration of forever, defined herein as: all the years ahead, every ordinary Tuesday, every hard month, and every moment in between. Termination of this agreement is not permitted. The Devoted One has reviewed the exit clauses and found them to be unacceptable. There are none.

Article IV — Governing Principles

This agreement shall be governed by the laws of love, common decency, and the mutual understanding that they are each other's person. In the event of any dispute, the matter shall be resolved by one (1) long hug and the acknowledgment that being together is more important than being right.
